FREQUENT QUESTIONS

Before you ask.

Is the VANTA GT a real production car?

No — the GT is a concept vehicle and this site is a design template. Every render, spec, and price is illustrative. Swap in your own brand, assets, and copy before launch.

What does a reservation actually commit me to?

Nothing binding. A reservation holds your place in line with a fully refundable deposit. You only enter a purchase agreement later, once specification and pricing are confirmed — and you can cancel for a full refund until then.

How is the refundable deposit handled?

In production you would route deposits through a regulated payment processor on a separate encrypted page. This template captures the reservation intent and confirmation number; wire your processor of choice to the form endpoint.

When do first deliveries begin?

Delivery windows are assigned in reservation order. The earlier your slot, the earlier your window. Your specialist confirms timing once your build is locked.

Can I change my finish after reserving?

Yes. Your configuration stays editable right up until you place a binding order. Reach your specialist or revisit the configurator anytime.

Where are you based?

The VANTA flagship studio is the address listed here. As a concept brand it is illustrative — replace it with your real location and hours before going live.
