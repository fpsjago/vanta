This Privacy Policy explains how VANTA ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you visit this site, configure a vehicle, or place a reservation.

## Information we collect

We collect the information you give us directly: your name, email address, phone number, postal or regional location, and the configuration details of any VANTA GT you build or reserve. When you submit a reservation, we also process the payment information required to take your deposit. We collect basic technical data automatically — browser type, device, and pages viewed — to keep the site secure and to understand how it is used.

## How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, process and manage reservations, send you updates about your reservation and the VANTA GT programme, and improve our products and services. We do not sell your personal information. We will only send you marketing communications where you have agreed to receive them, and you can withdraw that consent at any time.

## Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate — payment processors, hosting and email providers, and analytics partners — and only to the extent they need it to perform their work for us. We may also disclose information where required by law.

## Data retention and your rights

We keep your information for as long as needed to manage your relationship with us or to meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymise it. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details on our Contact page.

## Cookies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, and measure performance. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings or any consent controls presented on the site.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.
